➤ In addition to the above incentives, the following seven components of subsidy are also available under the scheme:

  1. State Transport Subsidy on raw materials and finished goods
  2. Employment Cost Subsidy
  3. Reimbursement of Standard Certification Fees/ Charges
  4. Subsidy on fees paid for coverage of the Credit Guarantee Trust Fund for Micro and Small Enterprise (CGTMSE)
  5. Export Promotion Subsidy
  6. Subsidy for participation in fairs/ exhibitions
  7. Additional subsidy/incentive to ZED-certified industrial enterprises

➤ Reimbursement of power charges @Rs 5/- per unit is the highest rate of reimbursement on power among the North Eastern States.

➤ State transport subsidy on raw materials and finished goods is only provided in the state of Tripura among the North Eastern States.
