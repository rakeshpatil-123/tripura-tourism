Enterprise classification based on investment in plant & machinery and annual turnover:

Enterprise Category Investment in Plant & Annual Turnover
Micro Enterprises A micro enterprise, where the investment in plant and machinery or equipment does not exceed one crore rupees and turnover does not exceed five crore rupees.
Small Enterprises A small enterprise, where the investment in plant and machinery or equipment does not exceed ten crore rupees and turnover does not exceed fifty crore rupees.
Medium Enterprises A medium enterprise, where the investment in plant and machinery or equipment does not exceed fifty crore rupees and turnover does not exceed two hundred and fifty crore rupees.
Large Enterprises A large enterprise, where the investment in plant and machinery or equipment is more than fifty crore rupees and turnover is more than two hundred and fifty crore rupees.
